Section 1

Cats

The cat is a small, furry, carnivorous mammal often kept as a household pet for companionship. Known for their soft fur, grooming habits, and sharp, retractable claws, they have been domesticated for thousands of years. Cats are known for their independence, agility, and hunting skills, often forming close, comforting bonds with their human owners.

Section 2

rabbits

Rabbits are small, furry mammals known for their long ears, powerful hind legs, and short, fluffy tails, with many breeds acting as popular, affectionate pets that can live for 8-12 years with proper care. As herbivores, they thrive on a diet of hay, vegetables, and pellets, and are renowned for their quick hopping, burrowing habits, and high fertility.
